Zombeh Survival

Zombeh Survival is a zombie shooter game where... you shoot zombies. (Who woulda guessed?)

Fight your way through many unique zombies in currently only 1 map (classic) and defeat the (wip) boss!

This game is in its indev phase, and is a passion project with a team of friends.

We have plans to release alpha v0.1 by December 18th. Follow the event for more details!
